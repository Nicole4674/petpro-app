# PetPro Community Plan — v1

The plan for building PetPro's exclusive groomer community using
Circle.so as the platform. This is Phase 3 of PetPro's product
strategy, AFTER the AI launch and token system are live.

---

## THE VISION

Every PetPro subscriber gets access to a private, members-only
community of fellow groomers — built INTO the PetPro app experience,
not on Facebook, not on a separate Discord. This becomes PetPro's
deepest moat after the AI.

**Why this matters strategically:**
- The AI is PetPro's first moat — competitors can copy it
- The community is PetPro's REAL moat — emotional lock-in, network
  effects, and authentic relationships are nearly impossible to copy
- Once a groomer has 200 groomer friends in the community + Nicole the
  founder responding live, they will never switch to MoeGo or Gingr

**The marketing hook:**
> "Real Claude. Fair price. AND a community of groomers who actually
> get it. The only way in is the app."

---

## WHY CIRCLE.SO (and not Discord, Slack, Facebook)

**Discord:** Free, but the wrong tool. No native paid-user gating
without a custom bot. Lives outside PetPro entirely. Feels like a
gamer chat, not a professional tool. Doesn't integrate with the
PetPro app experience at all.

**Slack:** Designed for internal teams, not consumer communities.
Pricing scales aggressively per user. Will become expensive fast.

**Facebook Groups:** Toxic, distracting, mixed with personal life,
hijacked by sales pitches. Exactly what PetPro is fighting against.

**Circle.so:** Purpose-built for paid communities. SSO integration.
Embeddable in PetPro. Customizable to brand. Mobile + desktop apps.
Pricing scales with FEATURES not member count, so it stays affordable
forever. Used by Notion, Webflow, ConvertKit for similar use cases.

## THE ROLLOUT STAGES

### Stage 0 — Pre-launch (NOW)
Don't build community yet. AI launch + tokens come first. Community
needs critical mass to feel alive — empty community is worse than
no community.

### Stage 1 — Soft Launch (~25-50 paying PetPro users)
- Sign up for Circle Professional ($89/mo)
- Set up basic spaces:
  - **Welcome & Introductions**
  - **Tips & Tricks** (Nicole's daily wisdom drops)
  - **Marketing & Growth** (slow-day ideas, social media)
  - **Tough Clients & Stories** (the venting / support corner)
  - **Photo of the Day** (before/afters from members)
  - **Q&A** (groomer to groomer)
- SSO integration: groomer logs into PetPro → one-click into Circle
- Embed Circle inside the PetPro dashboard so it feels like part of
  the app (not a redirect to a different site)
- Nicole posts daily for the first month to seed conversation

### Stage 2 — Growth (~50-200 paying users)
- Upgrade to Circle Business ($199/mo)
- Unlock automations (welcome new members automatically with a
  Nicole video)
- Add more spaces:
  - **Mobile Groomers** (subgroup for mobile-only shops)
  - **Boarding/Daycare** (subgroup for facility owners)
  - **Cat Groomers** (specialty subgroup)
  - **Live Events** (monthly Q&A with Nicole, expert guest groomers)
- Lower transaction fee (1% instead of 2%) helps if you ever sell
  paid courses or events through Circle

### Stage 3 — Scale (~500+ paying users)
- Upgrade to Circle Plus ($360+/mo)
- Get the **branded mobile app** — "PetPro Community" as its own
  app icon on phones
- AI agents in Circle for FAQ auto-replies + new member onboarding
- Custom SSO branding so the entire experience is PetPro
- This is when the community truly feels like a native PetPro feature

### Stage 4 — Optional Future
- If revenue justifies + community is the #1 driver of retention,
  consider building a custom in-app community (vs Circle)
- Honest take: probably never needed. Circle Plus at scale is still
  way cheaper than building + maintaining a custom community platform

## INTEGRATION POINTS WITH PETPRO

**1. Stripe-gated access.**
When a groomer subscribes to PetPro, a webhook adds them to Circle.
When they cancel, a webhook removes their Circle access. No leakage —
the community is truly gated to active subscribers.

**2. SSO so it feels seamless.**
Groomer logs into PetPro at trypetpro.com → click "Community" in the
PetPro sidebar → land in Circle already authenticated. Zero friction.

**3. Embed Circle inside the PetPro dashboard.**
Use Circle's embed widgets so the community lives INSIDE the PetPro
app, not as a separate destination. "The only way in is the app."

**4. Profile sync.**
Groomer's name, business name, and shop logo from PetPro automatically
populate their Circle profile. No double data entry.

**5. Activity-based AI nudges (advanced, later).**
When PetPro AI sees a groomer struggling with something (matted dog
client refusing a short cut, no-show fights, etc.), suggest:
*"Want to ask the community? Other groomers have dealt with this
exact scenario."* Drives community engagement organically.

## NICOLE'S ROLE IN THE COMMUNITY

Nicole's presence is what makes this work. Founders who post in their
own community see massively higher engagement and retention.

**Recommended cadence:**
- **Daily:** Quick post or comment somewhere (5-10 min/day)
- **Weekly:** One longer-form post (a story, a tip, a marketing idea)
- **Monthly:** Live Q&A or "Ask Me Anything" session
- **Ad-hoc:** Reply to questions in the Q&A space when she sees them

This is a couple hours per week TOTAL. Way less than running a
Facebook group, way more impact per minute.

**The tone Nicole sets:**
- Direct, warm, no corporate-speak
- Always has the groomer's back
- Shares her own real stories (the matting fights, the fired clients)
- Asks for feedback ALL the time — "what should PetPro build next?"

  Business cost in retention alone**

The ROI math is not even close. Circle pays for itself many times
over.

## OPEN QUESTIONS FOR LAUNCH

1. **First-month seeding plan** — how do we ensure conversation
   happens in week 1 so it doesn't feel dead? (Suggested: Nicole
   personally invites her first 25 groomer connections + posts 1-2
   times per day for the first 30 days.)

2. **Moderation policy** — what's bannable? (Spam, sales pitches
   from competitors, harassment, animal abuse content.) Need a
   simple community guidelines doc.

3. **Free trial users** — do they get community access during the
   30-day trial? (My take: YES. It's the wow factor that converts
   them to paid.)

4. **Cancellation grace period** — if a groomer cancels, do they
   lose access immediately or get 30 days? (My take: lose at end of
   billing period, not immediately. Don't be punitive.)

5. **Multi-staff shops** — does the owner's subscription give all
   their staff access? Or only the owner? (My take: Pro+ and Growing
   plans get N seats matching their staff count. Basic gets 1.)
